My Favourite Meal

My favourite meal is fried rice!

Just like the Nigerian jollof rice, Nigerian fried rice is one of the most popular choices in Nigeria. It is a very rich, simple yet delicious recipe with lots of fresh vegetables. We savor these meals ( jollof rice and fried rice ) so much that we can barely do without them at gatherings and festive seasons.

The ultimate foundation for the great taste in Nigerian fried rice is the chicken stock/broth so you need to get it right. Let's look at some of the ingredients used in the preparation of Nigerian fried rice.

Recipe for Nigerian Fried Rice
Ingredient Measurement
Rice ( preferably long grain rice ) 3 Cups
Vegetable oil 3 Caps
Chicken stock 3 Cups
Seasoning cube 2 cubes
Onion 1 bulb
Curry 1 Tablespoon
Thyme 1/2 Tablespoon
Ginger 1 medium piece
Garlic 4 cloves
Proteins Liver, Minced meat, Boneless chicken, Prawns or any protein of your choice
Vegetables 1/4 cups each of Carrots, Sweet corn, Red chilli pepper, green/red/yellow bell pepper, green beans, green peas, red peas, spring onion, etc.
Salt Add to taste
Note: There are various methods for preparing Nigerian fried rice. Also, it is not compulsory to make use of all the vegetables or proteins listed here. The major and common vegetables used are carrots, green beans or green pepper, red pepper and sweet corn.

Now that we have listed some of the ingredients, let's get cooking!!!

Steps

  • Wash the rice thoroughly before parboiling it. This is to remove dirt and some of the starch content from the rice.
  • Pour the vegetables in a bowl and season with thyme, curry powder, salt and seasoning cubes. Marinate for about 30 minutes.
  • Heat up the vegetable oil in a pan and pour the rice into the pan. Fry on high heat while stirring for about 3 minutes.
  • Add the chicken stock (to the level of the rice) and bring to a boil.
  • When the water in the rice has dried up, take it down and leave to cool.
  • Pour vegetable oil into a clean pan and heat it up.
  • Add chopped onion and leave to sizzle for about a minute. Add some garlic, ginger and the vegetables. Fry for about 4 minutes.
  • Add the protein of your choice and stir-fry for 1 minute.
  • Add the cooked rice in batches into the pan containing the vegetable mix. Stir and fry for about 3 minutes.
  • And voila! The food is ready!
